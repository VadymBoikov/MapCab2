A simulated real-time feed of geolocation data from San Francisco yellow cabs

The above map displays a simulated real time stream of geolocation data from 500 cabs in San Francisco. The stream is being played back in real time, based on historical geolocation data. A stream processing framework (Apache Storm) is used for transforming streaming data such that only the unoccupied cabs are displayed on the map. The goal of this app is to showcase a framework enabling a user to view real time cab status in order to catch an empty cab in their neighborhood by utilizing the locational data being constantly updated on the map. Further information available on the subsequent tabs and github.
